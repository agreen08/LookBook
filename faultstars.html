Description

Hazel has cancer. Despite the fact that the disease has temporarily receded, the girl does not feel a drop of joy. She goes to a support group, where one day she meets Augustus Waters and instantly falls in love with him. August and Hazel embark on a journey full of passion and life that will show them once again that the whole meaning of life can be found in any of its segments.

About the Author

Founded in 1947, Hal Leonard LLC has become the worlds largest source of music performance and instructional materials, with a catalog of over a million products in both physical and digital form. They represent many of the greatest songwriters and artists of all time and publish and distribute works from all genres of music for all styles of musicians and ensembles.
